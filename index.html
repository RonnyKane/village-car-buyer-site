Terms & Conditions

To be eligible for the $200 guarantee, the following criteria must be met:

  • Vehicle must be free and clear of any and all liens.
  • Vehicle must have clean Florida title free of all brands.
  • Competing offers must be in writing for a direct purchase-only agreement. (Trade-in valuations do NOT qualify.)
  • Offer must be from a licensed and bonded dealer. Private party sale comps do not qualify.
  • Vehicle must be drivable.
  • Seller must be located within 25 miles of Honest Autos.
