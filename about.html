Our Story

45 Years. One Community. One Promise.

Village Car Buyer was built on a simple idea: retirees deserve better than the dealership experience. We've seen too many of our neighbors get lowballed by dealers, wait a week for their check, or drive across town only to hear a below-market number. We decided to do something about it.

For over 45 years, we've been buying cars throughout Lake County and Sumter County, Florida. We know this community because we are this community — our team is made up of Villages residents who live, shop, and socialize right alongside you.

Our relationship with Citizens First Bank of The Villages means we pay cash — real, same-day cash — with no holds, no ACH delays, and no waiting around for a check to clear. When we shake hands on a deal, you walk away with money in your pocket that same afternoon.

We're licensed, bonded, and insured by the State of Florida. We back every offer with our $200 Beat-Any-Offer Guarantee. And we come to your front door so you never have to leave home.

That's the Village Car Buyer promise — and after 45 years, we've never broken it.
